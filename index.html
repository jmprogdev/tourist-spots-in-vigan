Why Visit Vigan?

Vigan City is located on the western coast of the island of Luzon in the province of Ilocos Sur, Philippines, stands as one of the few remaining Spanish colonial towns in the country. It was officially named as UNESCO World Heritage Site because of its historical charm remains so intact. Today, it is celebrated globally as one of the New Seven Wonders Cities of the World, allowing visitors to step back in time and experience the blend of Asian and European culture that defines its unique charm.
